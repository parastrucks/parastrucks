Our Story

Building businesses, one vehicle at a time

Paras Trucks commenced operations as an authorized Ashok Leyland dealer in January 2019, starting with our first 3S facility in Haryana. What began as a focused commitment to serve the commercial vehicle needs of the region quickly grew into a multi-state, multi-brand operation.

By November 2022, we expanded into Gujarat with Paras Trucks and Buses. In 2023, we became an authorized dealer for Switch Mobility — Ashok Leyland's EV subsidiary — bringing zero-emission electric vehicles to Haryana. And in 2024, we added HD Hyundai Construction Equipment to our portfolio, entering the construction and mining equipment segment.

Today, we operate 11 facilities across Haryana and Gujarat, backed by a team of 410+ professionals — serving fleet owners, transporters, infrastructure developers, and businesses with trucks, buses, EVs, and heavy machinery.

Our Haryana network offers the complete range of Ashok Leyland commercial vehicles — from LCV and ICV trucks to heavy-duty haulage, tippers, and buses — alongside Switch Mobility electric vehicles, all through five fully integrated 3S (Sales, Service & Spares) facilities in Hisar, Rohtak, Sirsa, Jind, and Charkhi Dadri.

We also operate four HD Hyundai Construction Equipment outlets — a flagship 3S facility in Hisar complemented by service-and-spares centres in Karnal, Rohtak, and Charkhi Dadri — serving the region's growing demand for excavators, wheel loaders, and heavy machinery.
